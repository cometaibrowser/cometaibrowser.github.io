FAQ

Why must I click your link and "Accept Invite"?

Eligibility for the promotional Pro period is evaluated when you enter through the invite link and complete the necessary steps. Skipping this action or entering through another path might not grant you the offer.

What to do if I can't open the invite link?

Switch network nodes (recommend US / SG), enable global mode; use incognito window; clear browser cache and cookies; try a different browser or device.

Can't see the Assistant entry?

Make sure you're using the same email from the invite; update Comet to the latest version and restart; check network quality and change nodes if necessary.
